# Description


In 1990 the German literary scholar Jürgen Hein published an article (Kanon-Diskussion in Literaturdidaktik und Öffentlichkeit Eine Bestandsaufnahme) for which he had compiled a fairly extensive collection of data: He collected data on course titles from severeal German universities and counted per author the number of courses dealing with them for the time between the summer semester 1970 and winter semester 1986/87. The data can serve as a very useful indicicator of a "historic" state of German author canonicity. 

In the table is a digital version of the table provided by Hein. Each row represents an author. The columns represent:

- GND-Nummer: the GND-ID of the author as identified by us
- Hein_Benennung: the name of the author as named by Hein
- Gesamt_count: the count of courses as listed by Hein (The numbers in brackets represent  the number of courses after 1980.)
- Epoche_Hein: the literary period as listet by Hein

When using the data, cite the following: Hein, Jürgen. „Kanon-Diskussion in Literaturdidaktik und Öffentlichkeit Eine Bestandsaufnahme“. In _Literaturdidaktik, Lektürekanon, Literaturunterricht_, herausgegeben von Gerd Labroisse. Amsterdamer Beiträge zur neueren Germanistik, 30 = 1990. Rodopi, 1990.


# Data collection

Jürgen Hein publishes the data compiled by him in the Appendix to his article. He writes the following about the acquisition:

> "Autoren-Kanon der Lehrveranstaltungen an Universitäten

> Ermittelt wurden die Ankündigungen aus den Vorlesungsverzeichnissen von 21 Universitäten und Gesamthochschulen von Sommer-Semester _1970 bis Winter-Semester 1986/87; pro Studienjahr wurde ein Semester herausgezogen. Die Zahlen lassen sich leicht auf das gesamte Studienjahr hochrechnen; die Angaben in Klammern beziehen sich auf Veranstaltungen nach 1980/81."

> ("Canon of authors in university courses

> Announcements from the course catalogs of 21 universities and Gesamthochschulen from the summer semester 1970 to the winter semester 1986/87 were examined; one semester was selected per academic year. The figures can easily be extrapolated to the entire academic year; the figures in parentheses refer to courses after 1980/81.")


We digitized the table provided by Hein. We manually linked the respective entities to the GND authority file.
