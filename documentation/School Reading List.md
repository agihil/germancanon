# School Reading List
## Description
School reading lists are official literature lists for German language teaching in secondary schools in all federal states of the Federal Republic of Germany [„offizielle[] Literaturlisten für den gymnasialen Deutschunterricht aller Bundesländer der Bundesrepublik Deutschland“] (Spitzer, Wortmann, and Wolfer, 2023).

There are two files: "schulleselisten.csv" and “schulleselisten_entries.csv”.

“schulleselisten_entries.csv” contains the data provided by Spitzer, Wortmann, and Wolfer (2023), available at https://www.owid.de/plus/lektemp2022/ (last accessed on November 13, 2025). It includes additional information on the authors’ gender, the literary texts, literary awards, and works of children’s and young adult literature. Further explanations are available in Spitzer, Wortmann, and Wolfer (2023): https://www.owid.de/plus/lektemp2022/LektEmp_InformationZurDatenbasis.pdf (last accessed on November 13, 2025).

Based on the data from Spitzer, Wortmann, and Wolfer (2023), we compiled the information relevant to our study in “schulleselisten.csv.”

The file "schulleselisten.csv" contains the following columns:
<ul>
<li>GND: GND identifier for the respective author.</li>
<li>SchulLeselisten_Autor: The author names are predominantly surnames. The spelling was adopted from Müller-Spitzer/Wortmann/Wolfer (2023). This does not necessarily have to match the spelling used in the GND.</li>
<li>SchulLeselisten_Baden-Württemberg</li>
<li>SchulLeselisten_Sachsen-Anhalt</li>
<li>SchulLeselisten_Hessen</li>
<li>SchulLeselisten_Hamburg</li>
<li>SchulLeselisten_Niedersachsen</li>
<li>SchulLeselisten_Rheinland-Pfalz</li>
<li>SchulLeselisten_Bayern</li>
<li>SchulLeselisten_Sachsen</li>
<li>SchulLeselisten_Saarland</li>
<li>SchulLeselisten_Mecklenburg-Vorpommern</li>
<li>SchulLeselisten_Bremen</li>
<li>SchulLeselisten_Brandenburg</li>
<li>SchulLeselisten_Nordrhein-Westfalen</li>
<li>SchulLeselisten_Summe: Frequency of occurrence in all school reading lists.</li> 
</ul>

When using the school reading list data, cite the following: Carolin Müller-Spitzer, Thomas Wortmann, Sascha Wolfer: Bildungsrelevante Lektüre war und ist vorwiegend von Männern verfasste Literatur.
